About

I have been working professionally with Flash since 1999, and have a high level of skill and a broad range of competencies.

Laterly I have also been working with 'HTML5' to fill those gaps created where Flash is not supported.

I have been making games, microsites, interactive marketing and laterly mobile PR materials for clients wanting to move into the new spaces offered by tablets and smartphones.

This work reflects my excitement at the possibilities of coded interfaces and animations, my specialisation as an actionscript developer, together with strong support skills that reflect the realities of an iPad lead digital space.

Also, I bake bread, maintain a small vegetable patch, and carve spoons to keep sane.
