Video Mosaic

Grab video frames to create a mosaic of the whole film - one frame captured 3 times a second.

Instructions:

  • Drag and drop a video file onto the drop area.
    • Something your browser can play. Try webm or mp4.
  • When the video controls become active, click play to start playback and capture.
  • Watch the video play, and the frames building the mosaic - this is realtime, so be patient.
  • When the process is complete, download your film as an image.
  • Experimental - Print resolution might not save, due to file size restrictions.
(This will restart the capture)
