Webcam chroma-key experiment

Grabbing a webcam video and compositing it with a static image based on a colour gate.

Change the RGB values of the colour gate with sliders.

Auto capture a series of images from the feed, and they will be turned into a GIF.
Same can be acheived by manually selecting frames to capture (use case would be a stop motion tool).

Additional options to adjust number of frames in the GIF, and auto-capture delay.
