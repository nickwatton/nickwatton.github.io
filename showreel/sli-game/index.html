HOW TO PLAY

You have 7 shots to hit a total of 6 drones. Each ball has the chance to hit multiple targets before it lands.
The ball follows the direction of the sliding arrow. Click “KICK” when you are ready take your shot.
The game is over when all drones are destroyed or you run out of balls.

 

How you collect points

Large drones - 2,500 points
Medium drones - 3,200 points
Small drones - 4,500 points

 

Bonus points

Accuracy - hit the drone as close to the centre as you can and you’ll be awarded an accuracy bonus.
Multiple–Hit - hit more than one drone with one ball and you’ll be awarded a multiple-hit bonus.
Time - hit all the drones within 30 seconds and you’ll be awarded a time bonus.
Remaining balls – pick up a bonus for any remaining balls you have left.
