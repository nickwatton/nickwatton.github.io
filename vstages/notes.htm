Notes to accompany the video

(The flickering on this video is due to limited access to output facilities, and was not present on the original design. The availability of either a camcorder with an adjustable shutter speed, or a video out board (to enable output direct from computer to video), would have avoided this problem.)

The video features my design for the installation / performance described above in the following four sections:

The design viewed as a traditional set model.

Sequence demonstrating the three stages in creating a 3D computer model:
'wire-frame' - used to make and assemble the model
'preview' - minimum detail required to show camera movement.
'rendered' - full detail including lights and colour.

'Walk through' of the set, showing 'film-projector', 'falling ball', flickering light from the 'film-projector', and 'rotating cube'.

... a final circling of the set, in virtual space.

The video is approximately 3 minutes long.
